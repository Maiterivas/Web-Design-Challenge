Humidity


Analyzing cities' humidity and their corresponding latitude, or location, it seems that there is not much correlation between the two variables. At a first glance, though, it seems that most values tend to stay over 20% humidity, except for a few values at both positive and negative 20 latitude. However, the vast majority of the cities in the dataset are above 60% humidity, regardless of the cities' specific latitude. Due to the number of outliers and poor correlation amongst the values, we still cannot draw a clear conclusion between a cities' latitude and how it affects its humidity.

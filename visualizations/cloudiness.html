Cloudiness


When comparing all city's cloudiness and latitude, there doesn't seem to be any kind of correlation between the two variables. Even when trying to make a deeper analysis regarding cities' cloudiness and latitude, all the datapoints seem to be scattered all over the plot in no particular order. Overall, when analyzing this plot we can see that it doesn't matter what the latitude is, whether negative or positive, the cloudiness on any given day of a city could range anywhere from a percentage of 0% to 100%.
